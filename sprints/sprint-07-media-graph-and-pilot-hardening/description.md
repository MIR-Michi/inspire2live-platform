# Sprint 07 — Media Graph & Pilot Hardening

**Phase:** 2 draft backlog
**Status:** Not Started

## Goal

Deepen the media and operations layer with direct SharePoint/Teams support, stronger observability, and pilot-driven hardening.

## Rationale

After the first pilot cycle, the most valuable hardening work is likely to sit around media retrieval, meeting artifacts, and the operational reporting needed to scale beyond one coordinator.

## Draft acceptance criteria

- SharePoint browse-and-link replaces the manual paste-only recovery path where approved.
- Teams meeting links and recordings can be attached without manual copy/paste.
- Communications usage metrics and pilot-report dashboards exist for ongoing review.
- Pilot feedback from Sprint 04 is triaged into shipped fixes or deferred backlog items.
- Sensitive comms data paths remain fully auditable after the new integrations.
